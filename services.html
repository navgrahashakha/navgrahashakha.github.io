The Process

How a Session Works

01

Book & Share Your Details

Fill out the contact form with your preferred service, date of birth (and time if known), and a little about what you're seeking. Gift sessions are also available — mention it in your message. I'll confirm within 24–48 hours.

02

I Prepare Your Reading

Before our session, I spend time with your chart or selected tarot spread — arriving fully prepared so our time together is rich and focused.

03

We Meet (Online or in Person)

Sessions are held online via Zoom or Google Meet, or in person in Goa. I hold the space with warmth and depth — this is a conversation, not a performance.

04

You Receive a Recording & Notes

Most sessions are recorded so you can return to them. You'll also receive any relevant charts or written notes to support your integration.
