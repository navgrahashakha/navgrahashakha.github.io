About Rushali

Bridging Stars & Soul

I am Rushali, an astrologer, tarot reader, and psycho-spiritual coach rooted in Goa, India. My practice weaves together the ancient language of the nine planets (Navgraha) with the archetypal wisdom of Jungian psychology.

Through years of studying the cosmos and the inner landscape, I have come to see astrology not as destiny, but as a map — one that helps us understand our patterns, wounds, and gifts with profound compassion.

Whether through a birth chart reading, a tarot spread, or one-on-one coaching, my work invites you to turn inward, reclaim your power, and align with your soul's authentic path.
