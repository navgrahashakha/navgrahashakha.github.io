I am Rushali — an astrologer, tarot reader, and spiritual coach rooted in Goa, India. My practice weaves together the ancient wisdom of the nine planets (Navgraha) with the intuitive language of tarot to help people find answers to the questions that keep them awake at night.

I came to astrology not through a classroom, but through a period of deep personal questioning — a time when nothing in my life made sense and I desperately needed to understand why. Astrology gave me that. It didn't just predict — it explained. And that changed everything for me.

Originally from Lucknow, I have spent years as a digital marketer — someone who lives in data, patterns, and systems. Astrology, I discovered, is the oldest system of pattern recognition there is. The two worlds are not so different.

The Journey

How I Got Here

01
☉

The Question That Started Everything

Like most people who find astrology, I didn't seek it out — it found me. During one of the most challenging periods of my life, I kept asking the same question: why is this happening to me? I had tried logic. I had tried ignoring it. Nothing gave me the clarity I needed. Then I found astrology — specifically, Vedic astrology and the ancient text of BPHS — and for the first time, the patterns of my life began to make sense.

02
☽

The Study — Self-Taught & Soul-Led

I learned astrology the way most serious practitioners do — obsessively and independently. Through online courses, ancient texts, and hundreds of hours of chart study, I built my knowledge from the ground up. Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS), the foundational classical text of Vedic astrology, became my primary guide. Alongside this, tarot entered my life as the perfect complement — where astrology gives the map, tarot gives the moment-to-moment conversation with the soul.

03
✦

The Readings — Skeptics Welcome

Over 50 readings later, I've noticed something: the people who come in most skeptical leave the most amazed. That's not a boast — it's actually my favourite part of this work. When someone walks in thinking "this is probably nonsense" and walks out saying "how did you know that" — that's when I know astrology is doing what it's supposed to do. It's not magic. It's an ancient intelligence that we've forgotten how to read.

04
🌙

Astrology as a Tool, Not a Verdict

I want to be clear about how I work: I do not practice predictive astrology as fortune-telling. For me, the birth chart is not a verdict — it is a map. A tool for understanding your patterns, your karma, your dharma, and the timing of your life's seasons. Yes, my predictions are often accurate. But more importantly, they help people understand why — and that understanding is what creates real change.

How I Work

My Approach

01

The Chart as a Living Map

I read your birth chart not as a fixed destiny but as a living, breathing reflection of who you are and where you're headed. Life is a blend of free will and what is written — and I honour both.

02

Honesty Over Comfort

I will not tell you what you want to hear. I will tell you what the chart says — with compassion, but without sugar-coating. The people who get the most from a reading are those who are ready for the truth.

03

Understanding Over Prediction

My goal is never to make you dependent on readings. It's to give you enough understanding of your own chart that you can begin to read your own life — its patterns, its timing, its deeper purpose.
