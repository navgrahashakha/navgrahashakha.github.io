My Story

Bridging Stars & Soul

I am Rushali, an astrologer, a tarot reader, and a spiritual coach rooted in Goa, India. My practice weaves together the ancient language of the nine planets — the Navgraha — with the archetypal wisdom of tarot.

My journey into astrology began long before it became a vocation. It started as a deep fascination with patterns, cycles, and the quiet intelligence behind life's timing. Over years of study and practice, I found that the birth chart is not a fixed fate but a living map — one that shifts meaning as we grow. It is a blend of free will and what is destined.

Alongside astrology, I fell in love with tarot as a language of the soul. The cards offered something charts couldn't — immediacy, imagery, and a direct line to the intuitive mind. Together, these two tools became the foundation of my work.

Today, my practice centres on Vedic astrology and tarot — two ancient tools that, together, offer both the broad cosmic map and the intimate symbolic mirror we need to navigate life with clarity and intention.
