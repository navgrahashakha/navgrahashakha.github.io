My Story

Bridging Stars & Soul

I am Rushali, an astrologer, tarot reader, and psycho-spiritual coach rooted in Goa, India. My practice weaves together the ancient language of the nine planets (Navgraha) with the archetypal wisdom of Jungian psychology.

My journey into astrology began long before it became a vocation — it started as a deep fascination with patterns, cycles, and the quiet intelligence behind life's timing. Over years of study and practice, I found that the birth chart is not a fixed fate but a living map — one that shifts meaning as we grow.

Alongside astrology, I fell in love with tarot as a language of the unconscious. The cards offered something charts couldn't — immediacy, imagery, and a direct line to the intuitive mind. Together, these two tools became the foundation of my work.

Today, my practice centres on Vedic astrology and tarot — two ancient tools that, together, offer both the broad cosmic map and the intimate symbolic mirror we need to navigate life with clarity and intention.
